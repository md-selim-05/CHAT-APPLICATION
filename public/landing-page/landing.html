ABOUT
  • Skip The Long Sign-Up Process
  • Zero Tracking For Absolute Privacy
  • Fully Automated Temporary Chat Rooms
  • Total Anonymity With Every Message
  • Flawless Simultaneous Multi User Chat

Jump straight into the conversation. We never ask for emails, passwords, or phone numbers. Open the app, join a room, and start chatting instantly.

Your data is yours alone. We never track your IP, store your messages, or monitor your activity. What happens in the room stays there.

Rooms exist only as long as you need them. The moment the last person leaves, the room and all its messages vanish into thin air.

Express yourself without hesitation. Pick a random nickname and speak your mind freely. No one knows your identity, keeping you safe and hidden.

Bring everyone together in one place. Our architecture supports dozens of active users, ensuring smooth group conversations without dropped messages or lag.
